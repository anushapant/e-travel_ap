Need Special Assistance?

Fret not! At Travoyage, we take care of your needs before you even think of them!

  • Senior Citizens

Our Services

Any passenger aged 60 years or above can avail the "Senior Citizen Special Assistance". The following will be provided in the form of assistance:

  • A wheelchair with an attendant
  • A loader to assist with luggage
  • Priority Check in
  • Priority Boarding and Deboarding
  • Assistance with stowing and retrieving baggage

Charges

At Travoyage we are committed to making our senior customers’ travel experience enjoyable and comfortable. There are no additional charges to avail special assistance for senior citizens.


Our Services

Any passenger with a mobility disability can avail special assistance under this category. The following will be provided in the form of assistance:

  • A wheelchair with an attendant
  • A loader to assist with luggage
  • Priority Check in
  • Priority Boarding and Deboarding
  • Assistance with stowing and retrieving baggage
  • Individual safety briefing by the Cabin Crew

Charges

At Travoyage we strive to make our special customers’ travel experience most comfortable. There are no additional charges for availing special assistance in this category.

  • A loader to assist with luggage
  • A Travoyage escort to assist the minor with check-in, security checks, immigration and customs

  • Priority Boarding and Deboarding
  • Assistance with stowing and retrieving baggage
  • Individual safety briefing by the Cabin Crew

Charges

At Travoyage we are committed to making our young customers’ travel experience most comfortable. In order to provide a good experience, we charge a fee of $25 to avail special assistance in this category.
